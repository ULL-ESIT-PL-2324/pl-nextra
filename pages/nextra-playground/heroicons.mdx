# Heroicons

The [heroicons page](https://heroicons.com/) is a great source of icons for your pages.
The `width` attribute helps to fix the size of the icon.

```jsx {7}
<svg
  className="h-6 w-6 text-gray-500"
  fill="none"
  viewBox="0 0 24 24"
  stroke="currentColor"
  strokeWidth="2"
  width="50" 
>
  <path
    strokeLinecap="round"
    strokeLinejoin="round"
    d="M12 8v4l3 3m6-3a9 9 0 11-18 0 9 9 0 0118 0z"
  />
</svg>
```
